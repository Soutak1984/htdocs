          Tariffs on Importing Electronic Components from China

          When you import electronic components from China, you have to pay customs fees or tariffs. Customs exemptions are available in a number of jurisdictions. However, some countries like United States of America have strict customs regulations in place.

          When electronic parts are shipped from China to the United Kingdom, the vast majority of them are subject to customs inspection. Customs is also known by other names such as import tax, customs tariff and import tariff. Understanding the tariff, also known as the HS code, is necessary in order to calculate the exact cost of customs.

          Customs duties are typically calculated based on the type of the electronics and the value of the shipment. Customs clearance is generally waived if the total value of the shipment is less than a certain threshold. You can outsource your shipping needs to a reputable company that have the necessary expertise to deal with all of the customs.

          How to Ship Electronic Components from China

          There are several shipment methods when you buy electronic components from China. Some methods are quicker and some methods are cheaper for bulk purchase.

          Sea Freight Shipping

          Sea Freight Shipping is the most cost effective method of transportation. It is mostly appropriate for large-scale shipments and makes the final price of your electronic components cheaper. However, this method requires a significant amount of time. It might take from a week to couple of months to be completed. As a result, only use this channel if you have a lot of free time.

          Air Freight Shipping

          Shipment by air freight is the most expedient and safest method to ship electronic components from China. This is, however, the most costly delivery method available. When you want something immediately and have the financial means to pay the fees, this is the route to choose.

          Rail Shipping

          Railway networks are extended and well distributed around the world. You may use this channel to have electronic componente transported to the places in your vicinity. Rail freight is not prohibitively costly, you would be able to deliver your shipment to the local train station to be transported to your destination.

          Door to Door Shipping

          This method is suitable for small to medium-sized deliveries, and it also includes insurance coverage.
