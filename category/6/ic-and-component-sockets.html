        Importance of Integrated Circuit(IC)

        With the invention of IC, a great revolution took place in the electronics industry. The invention of this piece accelerated the development of electronic boards. An IC or integrated circuit is a component that is made of semiconductor material and has small dimensions. But what defines the general definition of this component is the existence of different components in the IC. There are various components in the IC such as capacitors, transistors and inductors that make you use all of these components in smaller dimensions. By purchasing ICs and using them in your electrical circuits, you can significantly reduce the overall volume of your board. If you need to buy integrated circuit directly from high-grade manufacturers, and receive great after-sale service while paying the lowest possible costs, Yougou Electronics is the best Chinese supplier to work with.

        IC applications

        Different ICs perform different tasks on electronic boards. The same mobile phone you have in your hand is made up of several different ICs, each of which does some work on your smartphone. For example, the charging IC of your device is responsible for controlling the charging operation of the battery. Some important applications of IC are listed bellow:

        • Optocoupler
        • Amplifier
        • Controller
        • Microcontroller
        • Attenuator
        • Voltage regulator
        • Converter
        • Logical gates such as AND,...
