        Why should you buy Relay

        There are several parts in an electronic device that should match with each other to make their duties well. The relay is one of these parts that exist in almost all household appliances. The high application of this piece has caused a wide range of electronics. In general, the relay is a means of controlling high voltage flow between the two points from the circuit. Relays have different types and are produced by various brands.

        Applications of Relay

        By buying a relay, you can use this part in various devices. When some electronic devices have problems, the problem may be with this part or the switch of the device. If you have proper technical knowledge, you can easily replace these parts and reduce the cost of repairing your device significantly. Relays have very high applications and are made in different models. In this part of the article, we will introduce you to the users of all types of relays so that you can get to know more about this important part.

        • • Thermostats are a type of thermal relay that changes shape due to temperature change of the metal inside the relay. This part, which has many industrial applications, protects industrial devices by cutting off the electricity flow in case of temperature rise
        • • Some relays use a hydraulic system to cut and connect the current, when the electric current is connected to this relay, the oil in the relay moves and the current is cut until the oil returns to its original place.
        • •In electric motors, there is a relay called phase control, which checks the voltage before starting work so that no problem occurs during the operation of the motor.
        • • All electrical appliances in your home, such as irons, tea makers, refrigerators, and electric heaters, all have an electronic relay to cut off the electrical current of the device.
        • • Fuses will burn in case of unauthorized voltage and current passing through the circuit to cut off the current, by purchasing a magnetic relay you can use this piece as a fuse, with the difference that the magnetic relay does not burn and only cuts off the current.
